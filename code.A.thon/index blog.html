what prompt up my interest

My journey into web design began with a fascination for the internet and a curiosity about how websites are built. I remember spending countless hours exploring different websites, captivated by their design and functionality.
It was like uncovering a new world with every click. My interest grew when I started experimenting with creating my own simple web pages.
I was immediately hooked by the process of combining creativity with technology to build something functional and visually appealing.
As I delved deeper, I took online courses, practiced coding, and explored design principles, each step fueling my passion even more. What truly solidified my love for web design was realizing the impact a well-designed website can have on a user’s experience. The ability to blend aesthetics with usability, and to create something that not only looks great but also works seamlessly, was incredibly rewarding.
That’s when I decided to turn my passion into a career, and I’ve been dedicated to honing my skills and bringing unique digital experiences to life ever since.
